Benefits of E-Way Bills for Local Transporters in Bihar

For local transporters in Bihar, staying compliant with GST regulations is crucial—and the E-Way Bill system makes it easier than ever. From reducing delays to cutting down on paperwork, here’s how E-Way Bills can transform logistics for transporters.

What is an E-Way Bill?

An E-Way Bill is a digital document required for the movement of goods worth more than ₹50,000. It is generated electronically via the government portal and is essential for ensuring tax compliance under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime.

In Bihar, for intra-state transport, the E-Way Bill is mandatory if the value of the goods exceeds ₹1,00,000. This document includes details like the origin, destination, invoice number, transporter ID, and vehicle details.
